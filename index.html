10-Day Learning Plan

Master AI & Career Growth

A structured 10-day plan to learn AI tools, resume building, and LinkedIn optimization.

Day 1

Intro to AI & LLMs

  • Learn what AI, ML, and DL are, their differences, and real-world use cases.

  • Tools: ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity.
